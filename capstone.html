CAPSTONE PROJECT

L.O.K.A.L: Pagkilala sa Negosyong Malapit sa Iyo – Development of a Web and Mobile Application with IoT-Enabled Connected Systems for Tracking Small Businesses in Local Communities

Discussion of the Features

L.O.K.A.L. is a business app made specifically for small, rising, and start-up businesses within the San Lorenzo Subdivision community. It helps these neighborhood shops grow, get seen, and be appreciated by the locals. When signing up, people can choose to register as either a store owner or a customer. Store owners can easily share their business information, pin their exact location inside the subdivision, and list their products. Customers can then use the app's map and directory to easily find and buy from them. This simple setup connects the neighborhood together, making it easy for residents to support and buy from businesses right inside their own community.
